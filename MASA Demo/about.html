Our Story

MASA was founded in 2020 with a vision to showcase the beauty and quality of Egyptian textiles and fashion to the world. Our name, MASA, is inspired by the ancient Egyptian word for "journey" – reflecting our commitment to taking our customers on a journey through Egypt's rich textile heritage.

What began as a small boutique in Cairo has grown into a beloved brand that celebrates Egyptian craftsmanship and design. We work directly with local artisans and manufacturers to create clothing that honors traditional techniques while embracing modern aesthetics.

Our founder, Amira Hassan, a textile engineer with a passion for preserving Egyptian craftsmanship, established MASA to create a bridge between Egypt's rich textile heritage and contemporary fashion needs. Today, MASA stands as a testament to the beauty of Egyptian design and the quality of Egyptian cotton.

Our Mission

At MASA, our mission is to celebrate and promote Egyptian craftsmanship through high-quality, ethically produced clothing that honors our cultural heritage while embracing modern design sensibilities.

We are committed to:
