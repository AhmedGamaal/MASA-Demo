Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more

We accept credit/debit cards, Vodafone Cash, Fawry, and cash on delivery for orders within Egypt. For international orders, we accept credit/debit cards and PayPal.

How long does shipping take?

expand_more

For orders within Cairo, delivery typically takes 2-3 business days. For other cities in Egypt, it takes 3-5 business days. International shipping varies by location and typically takes 7-14 business days.

What is your return policy?

expand_more

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in their original condition with tags attached. Please note that customized items cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Do you offer international shipping?

expand_more

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please check our shipping page for more details.

How can I track my order?

expand_more

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the courier's website.
